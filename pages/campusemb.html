CAMPUS AMBASSADOR PROGRAM

IBM SkillsBuild offers you this exciting opportunity to be a part of the 6-month Campus Ambassador Internship Program. Be that champion who is the face and voice of IBM SkillsBuild at your college. Bring out the leadership skills in you and help us build the largest community of passionate learners about upskilling themselves on IBM SkillsBuild.

Roles and Responsibilities:
  • Building a network of students and teachers in your college using offline and online strategies.
  • Ideating and executing engaging campaigns in your institute.
  • Leading and organizing events related to IBM skillsbuild platform.
  • Collecting feedback/Impact Stories from students and other focus groups to improve learning strategies
  • Motivate and encourage students to take up online courses to upskill themselves.
  • Participating in webinars/capacity-building workshops organized by CSRBOX on monthly basis.
Register Now!
What will you Gain?
  • Accolades
    LOR and Completion Certificate of Internship.
  • Exposure
    Gain exposure to real-time problems and hands-on experience in program management, data-management, team management skills, program management and implementation
  • Capacity building
    Free monthly master class webinars from industry experts on professional soft skills, rapport building, leadership, Professional communication skills, Critical thinking and Problem-solving skills, agile skills, Project Management, and so on.
  • Branding
    Learn social media growth hacks, online branding, and promotion strategies.
  • Placement
    Placement assistance and early access to our new initiatives
  • Networking
    Foster your professional network with the one-on-one mentoring session.
Register Now!
project results
  • Currently pursuing Graduation or Postgraduate in 2nd or 3rd year.
  • Available for 3 months duration
  • Good written and oral communication skills and ability to plan, organize and conduct activities (addedadvantage).
  • Proactive, passionate, and adapt independently
  • Interested in learning about real-time project management and implementation
